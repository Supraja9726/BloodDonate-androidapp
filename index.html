Blood Donations

Have you at anytime witnessed a relative of yours or a close friend searching frantically for a blood donor, when blood banks say out of stock, the donors in mind are out of reach and the time keeps ticking? Have you witnessed loss of life for the only reason that a donor was not available at the most needed hour? Is it something that we as a society can do nothing to prevent? This thought laid our foundation.

Our mission is to fulfill every blood request in the country with a promising web portal and motivated individuals who are willing to donate blood.
"Time saving for you, life saving for us."

Most people can give blood. You can give blood if you:

  • are fit and healthy
  • weigh between 7 stone 12 lbs and 25 stone, or 50kg and 160kg
  • are aged between 17 and 66 (or 70 if you have given blood before)
  • are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years


About the Application: "Donate Blood" Application's ultimate aim is to focus on the people who are in need of blood and connect people with the willingness to do the same.

Food Donations

Food wastage is a major issue today which needs to be solved at the earliest possible. There is an alarming situation emerging throughout on hunger and starvation while food goes waste on the other side.

We focus on directing the food in surplus to orphanages and non- profit organizations which are in need of the food. We collect excess good food which would otherwise be discarded. This excess food is distributed directly to local charities supporting the community's most disadvantaged people.
"If you can't feed hundred people, at least feed one."

About the Application: "Donate Food" application mainly focusses on the people who are in need of food by encouraging people to do the same.
